Other Resources

The following books have their entire content available online:

  • Apache Geronimo: J2EE Development and Deployment by Aaron Mulder.
    Updating for Geronimo 1.0 final
  • Estudio del servidor Geronimo y desarrollo de aplicaciones prácticas by Katia Aresti González.
    Covers Geronimo 1.0 Milestone 3; released in Spanish under the Creative Commons license.

A number of print books covering Geronimo are planned or in production.

  • Apache Geronimo 2.1: Quick Reference by Vamsavardhana Reddy Chillakuru and Manu T. George.
    Packt Publishing; ISBN 1847196942
  • Geronimo: A Developer's Notebook by David Blevins.
    O'Reilly; First two chapters available at java.net
  • Professional Apache Geronimo by Jeff Genender, Bruce Snyder, and Sing Li.
    Wiley; ISBN 0471785431
  • Geronimo Live by Tom McQueeney
    SourceBeat
  • Apache Geronimo: J2EE Development and Deployment by Aaron Mulder
    Addison Wesley; ISBN 0321334833
  • Geronimo - Apache Geronimo im Einsatz (German) by Christian Dedek and Kristian Kohler.
    entwickler.press; ISBN 3-935042-97-3
  • Pro Apache Geronimo by Kishore Kumar
    Apress; ISBN 1-59059-642-0
  • Apache Geronimo:Handbuch für den Java-Applikationsserver by Frank Pientka
    DPUNKT-Verlag; ISBN 978-3-89864-517-1
