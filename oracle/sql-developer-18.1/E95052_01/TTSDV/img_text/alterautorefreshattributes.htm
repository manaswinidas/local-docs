This figure shows the Autorefresh cache options dialog. In the top left of the dialog are the Prompts and SQL tabs. In the middle left of the dialog are the autorefresh attributes. The owner of the cache group is shown and below the owner, the cache group name is shown. The Refresh Interval field is below the cache group name. To the right of the refresh interval field is a box that allows you to enter the interval. To the right of this box is a minutes menu. No selection is displayed. Below the refresh interval field is the refresh state field. To the right of this field are 3 choices shown as circles. You click in these circles. The choices are Paused, On, Off. At the bottom of the dialog, are the Apply and Cancel buttons.
