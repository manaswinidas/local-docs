This figure show the cache group unload/refresh with where clause dialog. There are two tabs at the top: Prompts and SQL. Below the tabs are the Owner and Name of the cache group. Below the owner and name is a box for you to enter an optional where clause. At the bottom of the dialog are Apply and Cancel buttons. Choose Apply to unload/refresh the cache group.
