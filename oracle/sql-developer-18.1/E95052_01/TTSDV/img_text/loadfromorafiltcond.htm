This figure shows the Filter dialog. At the center of the dialog there is a drop-down list for the Table name. The options are =, <>, LIKE and NOT LIKE. To the right of the drop-down list there is a text field for the filter pattern. At the bottom of the dialog are the OK and Cancel buttons.
