This figure shows the update statistics dialog. In the top left of the dialog are the Prompts and SQL tabs. In the middle left of the dialog is the owner field. The owner name is displayed to the right of the owner field. Below the owner field is the name field. To the right of the name field is the name of the table. Below the name field is the Invalidate Referenced Commands field. To the right of this field is a check box. Below the Invalidate Referenced Commands field is the Interval type for table statistics. Below this field is a menu selection allowing you to select either complete interval or single interval. In the bottom, right of the dialog are the Apply and Cancel buttons.
